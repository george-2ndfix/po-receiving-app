1. Overview

The PO Receiving App is a web-based tool built for 2nd Fix Doors & Hardware to streamline receiving purchase orders, allocating stock, printing labels, reporting damage, relocating stock, and reporting faults. It connects directly to Simpro so all changes sync automatically.

What it replaces:

  • Manual PO receiving in Simpro
  • Paper-based stock tracking
  • Phone calls/texts to report faulty or damaged items
  • Manual label writing
  • Handwritten filing slips

3. Purchase Order Receiving

Step 1: Select the PO

  1. Tap Receive from PO on home screen
  2. You’ll see open POs from Simpro (PO number, supplier, item count)
  3. Tap the PO to receive

Step 2: Review Items

Each item shows: description, part number, qty ordered, qty already received, qty remaining.

Step 3: Receive Items

Option A — Barcode Scanning (Recommended): Tap barcode icon → scan with camera → adjust qty → Confirm

Option B — Manual Entry: Find item → tap it → enter qty → Confirm

Step 4: Complete

PO status updates in Simpro automatically. Partial receipts keep the PO open.

⚠️ Double-check quantities before confirming — they sync to Simpro immediately!

4. Damaged Goods Reporting

  1. While receiving a PO, tap the red ⚠️ Damaged button next to the item
  2. Take up to 5 photos of the damage
  3. Add notes describing the damage clearly
  4. Tap Submit

What happens automatically:

  • ✅ Emails the supplier with photos and damage details
  • ✅ Emails the customer about the damage and reorder
  • ✅ Updates PO status to “PO : Items Damaged”
  • ✅ Saves the report for tracking

Tip: Always include photos and be specific. Report immediately when you open the box.

5. Back Orders

During PO receiving, mark items as Back Order. The app updates PO status to “PO : Back Order” and notifies the customer automatically.

6. Stock Allocation & Job Materials

Step 1: Select items to allocate

After receiving, select items and tap “Allocate Selected →”

Step 2: Review Job Materials (job orders)

For job orders, the Job Materials screen shows all materials for that job, where existing items are stored, and a progress bar. The app suggests putting new items in the same location.

Step 3: Choose storage location

Accept the suggestion or choose a different location → Confirm Allocation

What happens:

  • Stock allocated in Simpro to chosen location
  • Delivery photos uploaded to the job with location in filename
  • Labels generated ready to print

Tip: Always allocate immediately. Check Job Materials to avoid splitting a job across multiple boxes.

7. Label Printing

Printer: Brother QL-810W | Labels: DK-2225 (38mm continuous)

  1. After allocating, tap Print Labels
  2. PDF opens with item labels + filing label (last page, job orders only)
  3. Print at Actual Size (never “Fit to Page”), portrait orientation
  4. Stick filing label on paper PO/pack slip → file in job bag

8. Relocate Stock

Move items between storage locations after receiving.

  1. Tap Relocate Stock on home screen
  2. Search by Job or PO number
  3. Tick items to move (greyed out ⏳ items can’t be moved yet)
  4. Choose new location → Confirm Move

The app handles the 3-step Simpro process automatically with rollback on failure.

9. Job Intel

Quickly look up job info without opening Simpro.

  1. Tap Job Intel or enter a job number
  2. See: job name, site address, contact details, status, notes

Use when you’re unsure which job stock belongs to, need a site contact number, or checking before dispatch.

10. Fault Reporting

  1. Tap Report Fault from the menu
  2. Name and email auto-filled from your login
  3. Enter: item description, PO/job number, fault description
  4. Add up to 3 photos (📷 Camera or 🖼️ Gallery)
  5. Tap Submit

An automatic investigation is triggered. George is notified. No manual follow-up needed.

11. Orders Email (orders@2ndfix.com.au)

Shared mailbox for all PO/supplier communications. All damage reports and back order notifications go from this address.

Access in Outlook: Profile icon → Open another mailbox → type orders@2ndfix.com.au

  • ✅ Reply to supplier/customer emails from orders@ when needed
  • ✅ Check if someone already replied first
  • ❌ Don’t use personal email for PO correspondence
  • ❌ Don’t delete emails from the shared mailbox
